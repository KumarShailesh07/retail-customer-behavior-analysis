# 🛍️ Retail Customer Behavior Analysis

> An end-to-end Data Analytics project built using Python, SQL Server, and Power BI to analyze customer purchasing behavior, identify revenue drivers, evaluate subscription adoption, and uncover opportunities for business growth.

---

# 📌 Project Overview

Retail businesses generate large volumes of customer transaction data every day. However, without proper analysis, it becomes difficult to understand customer purchasing patterns, identify high-value customer segments, evaluate product performance, and develop effective marketing strategies.

This project transforms raw customer transaction data into actionable business insights through a complete analytics workflow involving data cleaning and exploration in Python, business analysis using SQL, and interactive dashboard development in Power BI.

The objective is to help management understand customer behavior, optimize product strategies, improve customer engagement, and drive revenue growth through data-driven decision-making.

---

# ❓ Business Problem

The retail company collects customer transaction data but lacks visibility into the factors influencing customer purchasing behavior and overall business performance.

Management wants answers to several critical business questions:

* Which customer segments generate the highest revenue?
* Which product categories drive business growth?
* Do subscriptions influence customer spending behavior?
* Which customers are most valuable to the business?
* How effective are discounts in driving purchases?
* Which product categories require business intervention?
* What opportunities exist to increase revenue and customer retention?

Without these insights, marketing campaigns, inventory planning, customer engagement initiatives, and business strategies cannot be optimized effectively.

# 🔗 Key Business Findings

## 📈 Revenue Drivers

### Clothing Dominates Revenue Generation

* Clothing generates approximately **$104K** in revenue, making it the highest-performing category.
* Clothing also records the highest sales volume with **1,737 purchases**.

### Young Adults Drive Business Performance

* Young Adults contribute the highest revenue (**$62K**).
* This segment also records the highest purchase volume (**1,028 transactions**).
* Young Adults represent the most valuable customer segment for the business.

---

## 👥 Customer Behavior Insights

### Subscription Adoption Remains Low

* Approximately **73% of customers are not subscribed**.
* Only **27% of customers have an active subscription**.
* This indicates a significant opportunity to increase recurring revenue.

### Customer Loyalty Supports Subscription Growth

* Repeat buyers have a higher subscription rate (**27.56%**) than non-repeat buyers (**22.41%**).
* Loyal customers appear more willing to engage with subscription programs.

---

## 🛒 Product Insights

### Outerwear Underperforms

* Outerwear generates only **$19K** in revenue.
* It is the lowest-performing category in both revenue and sales volume.

### Discount Dependency

* Several products exhibit a high percentage of discounted purchases.
* This suggests that discounts play an important role in influencing customer purchasing decisions.

---

## 💰 Revenue Insights

### Non-Subscribers Generate More Revenue

* Subscribers and non-subscribers spend a similar amount per transaction (~$59).
* However, non-subscribers contribute significantly higher total revenue due to their larger customer base.

### Revenue Contribution by Age Group

| Age Group   | Revenue Contribution |
| ----------- | -------------------- |
| Young Adult | 26.66%               |
| Middle-aged | 25.33%               |
| Adult       | 24.09%               |
| Senior      | 23.92%               |

Young Adults contribute the highest share of total revenue.

# 💡 Strategic Recommendations

## 1️⃣ Increase Subscription Adoption

* Introduce loyalty rewards and subscriber-exclusive benefits.
* Offer personalized promotions to non-subscribers.
* Develop targeted campaigns to convert repeat buyers into subscribers.

## 2️⃣ Focus on High-Value Customer Segments

* Increase marketing efforts toward Young Adult customers.
* Build retention strategies around high-value customer groups.
* Personalize offers based on customer purchasing behavior.

## 3️⃣ Improve Outerwear Performance

* Introduce seasonal promotions.
* Bundle Outerwear products with Clothing purchases.
* Reassess pricing and product assortment strategies.

## 4️⃣ Leverage Clothing Category Success

* Maintain strong inventory availability.
* Expand product offerings.
* Implement cross-selling strategies with Accessories and Footwear.

## 5️⃣ Optimize Discount Strategies

* Identify products heavily dependent on discounts.
* Balance promotional offers with profitability objectives.
* Develop targeted discount campaigns instead of broad discounting.
